## Overall Portfolio Structure

Rather than presenting PromptPilot as **"Here's my app,"** present it as a complete case study:

1. **The Problem** — Why this product exists.
2. **Product Discovery** — Research, personas, and goals.
3. **UX Journey** — User flows and interaction decisions.
4. **Visual Design** — Design system and interface evolution.
5. **Architecture** — How the system is built.
6. **AI Pipeline** — How prompts are generated and refined.
7. **Implementation** — Frontend, backend, and infrastructure.
8. **Quality & Security** — Testing, monitoring, and protection.
9. **Results & Reflection** — What you learned, trade-offs you made, and future improvements.

This structure tells a compelling story: you didn't just build an application—you **conceived, designed, engineered, and evaluated** a complete product from end to end. That breadth is often more persuasive than showcasing isolated technical skills.
